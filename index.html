Common Questions / अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले सवाल
How long does document review take?
Documents की जाँच में कितना समय लगता है?
Usually 24–48 hours on business days. You'll get a notification when done.

आमतौर पर 24-48 घंटे (working days में)। जाँच होने पर notification आएगा।
What is MOV?
MOV (Minimum Order Value) क्या है?
MOV is the minimum cart value a retailer must reach before they can place an order from you. Example: if MOV = ₹500, the retailer must add at least ₹500 worth of your products before ordering.

MOV वो minimum amount है जो retailer को order करने से पहले cart में डालना होगा।
When will I receive payment?
Payment कब मिलेगी?
Payment is settled to your bank account 2 business days after the order is delivered (T+2).

Order deliver होने के 2 business days के बाद bank account में payment आएगी।
My product was rejected — why?
मेरा product reject क्यों हुआ?
Common reasons: wrong category, missing MRP, duplicate name, or missing brand. Open your Seller Store to fix the product directly there.

सामान्य कारण: गलत category, MRP missing, नाम पहले से है, या brand missing। Seller Store खोलें और वहाँ product ठीक करें।
What is FSSAI? Do I need it?
FSSAI क्या है? मुझे चाहिए?
FSSAI license is mandatory if you sell food or grocery items. Apply at fssai.gov.in. You can upload the acknowledgement slip while the license is being processed.

खाने का सामान बेचते हैं तो FSSAI license जरूरी है। fssai.gov.in पर apply करें। Processing के दौरान acknowledgement slip upload कर सकते हैं।
